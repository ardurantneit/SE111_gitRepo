Trivia

  • When Chuck and Lem first meet at the planetarium, Chuck tells Lem about his plans when arriving on their planet. In the Brazilian release, Chuck says he was planning on thrusting the American flag on the planet's soil, playing some golf and coming back to Earth on time for the Rio 2016 Olympics, because golf would be one of the selected sports. The movie was released in Brazil shortly after the International Olympic Committee (IOC) announced that the 2016 Olympics would be held in Rio de Janeiro.
  • All the female aliens wear dresses. The males wear shirts without pants.
  • Production lasted sixteen months, roughly half the amount of time it takes to make a Pixar movie.
